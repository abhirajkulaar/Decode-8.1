Journey so far!

The journey to launch has been one of endless toil from the team and an overwhelming support from all of you.

January,2021

Public Release

After overwhelming efforts by the team. Covine is released to public with a potential to save more than 2 million lives. One of the greatest medical achivement of 21st century.

December,2020

FDA Approval

Following months of strict independent studies and ferderal scrutiny the vaccine gets approved by the FDA for public release and administration.

August,2020

Phase 3 Human Trials

A prolonged study on a pool of more than 2000 patients is performed on the vaccine. The drug is evaluated to be beneficiial to more than 80& of the patients. Reportedly leading to a 70% lesser death rate per CoVid patient as compared to population not being administered with Covine-21.

August,2020

Phase 2 Human Trials

Phase 2 begins to explore the effectiveness of the drug. A total of 250 participants volunteer for the effort.

June,2020

Phase 1 Human Trials

The vaccine enters Phase 1 human trials with a pool of 50 human subjects to explore safety and dosing of the drug..

May,2020

Preclinical Trials

Covine 20 passes pre-clinical trials assuring safety of injection and is provisioned for initial limited human testing.
